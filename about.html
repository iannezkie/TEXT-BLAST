About Text Blast

Text Blast is a powerful messaging tool designed to streamline communication within organizations. It allows users to send bulk text messages to specific groups quickly and efficiently, ensuring important information reaches everyone who needs to know.

Features

  • Bulk Messaging: Send messages to multiple recipients at once.
  • Scheduled Messages: Plan and schedule messages to be sent at a later time.
  • Recipient Groups: Organize contacts into groups for easy management.
  • Message History: Keep track of sent messages and view a history of communications.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Simple and intuitive design for ease of use.

How It Works

1. Create or select a recipient group.
2. Compose your message (up to 150 characters).
3. Choose to send the message immediately or schedule it for later.
4. Click 'Send' and let Text Blast handle the rest.
